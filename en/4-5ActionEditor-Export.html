Pixels gap between small pictures that will synthesis the large pciture.

 

SortStyle

Sample

 

According to the specified width and height. Arrangement picture resource from left to right horizontally, return to left if reach the maximum width, repeat this process until all picture resources that match the condition have been inserted.

 

Normal

 

According to the specified width and height. Arrangement picture resource from left to right horizontally, when reach the maximum width, still fill picture unless the blank space can not hold, repeat this process until all picture resources that match the condition have been inserted.

 

Interliger

 

According to the specified width and height. Arrangement picture resource from left to right and up to down at the same time, picture resources will be inserted from left-up to right-down.

 

ExportFormat

 

Selects format to export. png or jpeg.
